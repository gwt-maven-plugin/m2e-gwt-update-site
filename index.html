m2e-gwt-update-site by gwt-maven-plugin

Update site for m2e-gwt project

This repository hosts P2 update site for the m2e-gwt project.

Two repositories will be available:

a working (snapshots) one under the http://gwt-maven-plugin.github.com/m2e-gwt-update-site/nightly URL.

a stable (releases) one under the http://gwt-maven-plugin.github.com/m2e-gwt-update-site/releases URL.
